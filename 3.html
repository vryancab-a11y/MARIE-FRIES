About Marie Fries 🍟

We're a Cebu-based fast food e-commerce brand passionate about delivering crispy, delicious food right to your doorstep. Started as a school project, Marie Fries grew into a full-featured food ordering platform.

Our Story

Marie Fries was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves great fast food at a fair price. We specialize in golden crispy fries, juicy smash burgers, and all-beef hotdogs — all prepared fresh for every order.

Our platform features a full inventory system, real-time order tracking, and a seamless checkout process — making ordering as satisfying as eating.
